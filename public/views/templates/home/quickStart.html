How to use Board State like a pro!

Step 1: Adding Cards to the Game Board

To add cards to your Board State, locate the Card Portal and start typing the name of the card you would like to display. Suggestions will start to populate in a drop-down and can be selected from there. The cards will always appear under the Card Portal, which can be moved around the screen to a position of your liking.

Cards are draggable as well but can be manipulated in several other ways too:
  • Double clicking them will tap the card.
  • Right clicking will bring up a context menu with many more options depending on the card's type.
  • Clicking will remove the card from the game board.

Step 2: Setting Additional Game Information

If you would like, you can display any information you would like about the current state.
  • Right/left clicking life totals will increase/decrease them.
  • Clicking a 'zone' in a player's stats area will open a panel where you can add cards to that zone.
  • The Stack is a zone where cards can be added to show what spells are currently resolving.
  • On the right side of the screen, the button will toggle a panel where you can write a description for the current state.
